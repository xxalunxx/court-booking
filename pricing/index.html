Common Questions

Do you take a cut of my bookings?

No. This is a flat monthly fee regardless of how much you book. You keep 100% of your booking revenue, minus PayMongo's own processing fee if you use that payment method.

How long does setup take?

Typically a few business days — we configure your courts, pricing, and payment methods, then hand you a live booking page.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time as your facility grows.

What happens to customer data?

It stays in your own dedicated database — never shared with other facilities on the platform.
